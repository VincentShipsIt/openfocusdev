# Pricing

## Free (Core OSS)

- **$0/month** forever
- Full task management
- Self-hosted
- All platforms (web, mobile, desktop)
- Community support

## Pro

- **$9/month** per user
- Everything in Free
- AI task agents (100 executions/month)
- Team workspaces (up to 10 members)
- Calendar & Slack integrations
- Email support

## Team

- **$19/month** per user
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited AI executions
- Unlimited team members
- Advanced permissions
- Priority support
- Custom integrations

## Enterprise

- **Custom pricing**
- Everything in Team
- SSO / SAML
- Dedicated infrastructure
- SLA guarantees
- Onboarding support

---

*TaskFlow Core is and always will be free and open-source. Cloud pricing funds continued OSS development.*
